Question:
In Figure 7.10, what indicates that Complex III contributes to the proton gradient across the membrane?
A) The presence of Cyt c only
B) The H+ arrow pointing upward
C) The location near the matrix
D) The connection to Complex IV
Explanation:
Visual Component: The diagram shows an H+ arrow pointing upward from Complex III into the intermembrane space.
Textual Component: The figure caption explains that protons are pumped from the mitochondrial matrix to the intermembrane space during electron transport.
Integration: Understanding this concept requires both seeing the H+ arrow in the visual and understanding its role in creating the proton gradient as described in the text. The correct answer is B.
